Towards Evaluating the Built-Environment for Accessibility: A Hybridized Approach for Identifying Buildings with Ramp Entrances

Data sources included:

OpenStreetMap (OSM) extract: Retrieved 794 polygons building=yes (random 400 + 394 “wheelchair=yes” AND “building=yes” tags).

Mapillary API requests: Retrieved total of ~12,810 images. First, by source (1) centroids. Then, to increase the dataset, images were retrieved from segments based on local knowledge.
